# 🔒 LOCK IN — The Mental Health Game

> *A choice-based mental health game for urban youth. Built to build.*

## What Is This?

**LOCK IN** is a browser-based, choice-driven mental health game designed for students navigating real life in urban school environments. Every scene puts you in a situation — drama, peer pressure, toxic people, social media spirals — and your choices determine your **Vibe Score**.

The core message: **You are in control of your own happiness.** Once you realize you can remove toxic people and avoid situations that drain you, everything changes.

---

## 🧠 The Psychology Behind It

Research shows:
- **93%** of school health workers report increased student anxiety since 2019
- Strong, positive peer relationships are one of the most powerful protective factors for youth mental health
- Boundaries, help-seeking, and self-regulation are learnable skills — not personality traits

This game teaches those skills through situations kids actually face.

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in any browser — no install needed
2. Navigate through **8 real-life school scenarios**
3. Make choices that affect your **Vibe Meter**
4. Hit 100 to unlock **Main Character** status
5. Learn the mental health principles behind every choice

---
